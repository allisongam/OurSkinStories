Our Skinfluencer Community

The Skinfluencer Community is a self-made community of influencers who are passionate about skincare and sharing their skincare journeys. Our community is dedicated to promoting skincare and sharing our knowledge and experiences with others. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced skincare expert, we're here to help everyone feel comfortable in their own skin. Join us on this journey to unlock the full potential of your skin.

"The Skinfluencer Community has been a valuable resource for me to learn about skincare and sharing my knowledge with others. I have been able to connect with a wide range of people who share my passion for skincare and seeking to empower others to achieve their skincare goals."

Pauline Choi is a skinfluencer who has been growing at a fast rate! She is Korean-American and loves to give reviews and share her experiences with skincare products from South Korea. She reviews many different products, always staying true. Her first viral video was on acne patches. This video received over 8 million views and gained over 1.5 million likes. Emma Itoh

Emma Itoh is a skinfluencer who is has been trending online recently! She is Japanese and loves to post skincare routine for all skin conerns such as acne-prone, hyperpigmented, dry, oily, or sensitive skin types. She shares her experiences with skincare products from Japan. Her first popular video was a routine for glass skin, a viral concept going on in the beauty community. Her video reached over 1.5 million views and gained over 194k likes. Her videos help people explore new products and create new routines!

Yuri Lee

Yuri Lee is a skinfluencer who is Korean and loves to promote healthy skincare routines! She is a skincare expert and has been growing online for a while. She has a multitude of skincare products and routine videos. Her first viral video was a routine using Gua Sha, a traditional Chinese method to improve blood circulation in the face and neck. Her video received over 17 million views and gained over 1.5 million likes. She is a huge influencer and inspires others to try out her favorite products, and includes her own and her family skincare routines where results speak for themselves!
